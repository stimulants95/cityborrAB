# Design Brief: Cityborr i Umeå AB

## Project Overview
- **Company:** Cityborr i Umeå AB
- **Industry:** Construction services (concrete drilling, sawing, demolition, remediation)
- **Website Purpose:** Modern redesign sketch/mockup to present to client - replacing outdated design while keeping all existing information
- **Target Audience:** Large construction companies (e.g., NCC, major contractors)
- **Project Scope:** Static website prototype in TypeScript

## Brand & Visual Direction

### Brand Feeling
- **Core aesthetic:** Industrial & robust with raw materials and concrete surfaces
- **Design approach:** Clean, professional B2B design with generous whitespace
- **Key attribute:** Animations that make the site feel alive and modern

### Visual References
- **Primary inspiration:** [Boliden.com](https://www.boliden.com/sv/)
  - What we like: Clean design, generous whitespace, subtle animations, professional B2B feel, restrained color palette, clear navigation
  - Modern minimalism that still feels substantial and trustworthy

### Existing Brand Materials
- **Logo:** Cityborr logga.jpg (white text with saw blade symbol, black background) - KEEP AS IS
- **Tagline:** "Din specialist på Betonghåltagning, sågning & rivning!"

### Color Palette
- **Base colors:** Black & White
- **Accent:** Concrete gray (industrial feel)
- **Approach:** Restrained, industrial - avoid too much color

### Typography
- **Style:** Modern sans-serif with industrial feel
- **Hierarchy:** Clear, clean, professional

### Imagery Style
- **Source:** Use existing project images from current website
- **Treatment:** Professional, showcase real work and capabilities
- **Approach:** Let the raw, industrial work speak for itself

### Visual Avoid
- Too much color (keep industrial palette)
- Overly playful or casual elements
- Anything that doesn't convey professionalism and capability to large contractors

## Design Principles

1. **Industrial Authenticity:** Raw materials, concrete aesthetic, real work showcased
2. **Clean Professionalism:** Generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, B2B credibility
3. **Modern Movement:** Subtle animations that bring life without distraction
4. **Restrained Palette:** Black, white, concrete gray - let the work be the color
5. **Information Integrity:** Keep all existing content - this is a visual upgrade

## Key Success Criteria

- Feels modern and industrial (not old-fashioned)
- Appeals to large construction companies
- Animations add life and engagement
- Clean, professional B2B aesthetic like Boliden
- Maintains all current information and structure
- Showcases capabilities through existing project imagery
